Objection-Busting Copy Inserts

Drop these lines into longer ad copy to pre-empt the most common objections before they kill conversions.

Objection

"Will clients actually pay?"

Counter

"People already pay lawyers, coaches, and advisors every day. They just need a frictionless way to pay you."

"What if it's complicated to set up?"

Counter

"Sign up, set a price, share your link. Most experts are live in under 10 minutes. No code. No tech knowledge required."

Counter

"You keep 78% — and Stripe's processing fee comes out of Convozo's 22%. You take home 78% clean, every time."

Objection (Client)

"How do I know the expert is qualified?"

Counter

"Every expert on Convozo has a verified professional profile. You can see their background, specialty, and response time before you pay."

Targeting Setup

Recommended Meta Ads audience configurations. Start broad, then narrow based on data.

🎯 Expert Audience — Cold

  • Age: 28–55
  • Interests: Legal services, Law, Consulting
  • Interests: Life coaching, Business coaching
  • Interests: Financial planning, Investment advice
  • Job titles: Lawyer, Attorney, Consultant, Advisor, Coach, Doctor
  • Behaviours: Small business owners
  • Exclude: People who already signed up (Custom Audience)

👤 Client Audience — Cold

  • Age: 25–50
  • Interests: Legal advice, Personal finance
  • Interests: Entrepreneurship, Startups
  • Interests: Health & wellness, Mental health
  • Behaviours: Online buyers (high value)
  • Income: Top 25% (Facebook allows this in some markets)
  • Lookalike: 1% of existing paying clients

🔁 Retargeting Audiences

  • Website visitors (last 30 days) — did not convert
  • Video viewers — watched 50%+ of any ad
  • IG Profile visitors (last 14 days)
  • FB Page engagers (last 14 days)
  • Custom audience: Email list upload (existing contacts)
  • Lookalike 1%: Based on all website visitors

🌍 Geo Targets (Priority)

  • 🇳🇬 Nigeria — Lagos, Abuja, PH (experts + clients)
  • 🇬🇧 UK — London, Manchester (high intent, high LTV)
  • 🇺🇸 USA — professional hubs (NY, LA, ATL)
  • 🇬🇭 Ghana — Accra
  • 🇰🇪 Kenya — Nairobi
  • Start with NG + GH — lower CPM, high conversion for expert supply

📐 Ad Placement

  • Instagram Feed — primary (highest CTR)
  • Instagram Stories — video/story formats only
  • Instagram Reels — video scripts V1/V2/V3
  • Facebook Feed — long copy ads (E1, E3, C2)
  • Facebook Reels — mirror IG Reels
  • Disable: Audience Network, Messenger (lower quality)

⚙️ Campaign Structure

  • Campaign: Conversion (not Reach or Traffic)
  • Optimise for: Lead or Purchase event (pixel required)
  • Budget: CBO (Campaign Budget Optimisation)
  • Ad sets: Max 4 per campaign
  • Ads per ad set: 3–5 creatives
  • Learning phase: Don't edit until 50 conversions/week

Budget Allocation & Scaling Plan

Start lean, validate, then scale only what's proven.

Scaling Rules

✓

Scale winning ad sets by 20% every 48–72 hours — not more. Rapid scaling resets the algorithm.

✓

Never edit a live winning ad. Duplicate it, then scale the duplicate.

✓

Pause any ad with CTR below 1% after $25 spend.

✓

Always have 2–3 new creatives in testing alongside scaled ads.

✓

Refresh creatives every 3–4 weeks to avoid audience fatigue.

✓

Install the Meta Pixel on convozo.com and track "Sign Up" as a conversion event — essential for optimisation.
