  • Call Bookings — Completion Threshold: If a session lasts at least 30% of the booked duration, the payment is captured in full. If the session does not meet this threshold, 50% of the session fee will be charged.
  • Client No-Show: If the Expert joins the session but the Client does not, a 30% no-show fee will be charged and the remaining authorization released. This compensates the Expert for their reserved time.
  • Expert No-Show: If the Client joins the session but the Expert does not, the full authorization is released and the Client is not charged.
